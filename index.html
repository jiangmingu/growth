Inspiration

My inspiration is the black truffle, which lives on oak and hazel roots. This nutritious plant kills the vegetation that feeds it until the spring after the truffle is dug up. Black truffles end up being dug up and eaten, but they can't live like trees.

I use the development of human embryos to illustrate the nutrients and resources we draw from the earth The growing round canvas above represents human destruction of the earth
